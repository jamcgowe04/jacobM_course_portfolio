Top 5 Animes

  1. Gurren Lagan

    2. A mecha adventure that follows humanity's fight for freedom, along with its themes to go beyond your limits and never give in.

  2. Akira(1988)

    3. This futuristic dystopian adventure goes into depth about government corruption, abuse of power, and consequnces that can lead to chaos.

  3. One Piece

    4. Follows Monkey D. Luffy and his quest to be the pirate king and his crew's search for the ledgendary treasure, the One Piece.

  4. Dragonball Z

    5. Follows Goku and His allies on an adventure to defend earth from every threat that comes their way. Filled with Epic fights the increasingly leaves room for self-improvement.

  5. Cowboy Bebop

    6. Space western anime that combines action, great jazz music, and smooth storytelling. All while exploring the personal lives of bounty hunters.

Why this is my top 5 is for the amount of emotion and ambition each one gives. They've also been the most memorable for me in all my years of watching.
